Not So

Everyday Jewelry · Made-to-Layer

Not So Basic makes minimal, stackable jewelry for everyday wear — rings, chains, and hoops made for lecture halls, late-night drives, and long weekends. Pinterest-coded, effortlessly layered, and never trying too hard. This is jewelry for the version of you that shows up daily, not just for special occasions.

Target Audience

College girls building their everyday stack between classes
Gen Z shoppers who screenshot Pinterest boards before they buy
Anyone who wants their initial, birthstone, or a tiny charm in gold
Concert and festival dressers layering pieces for a look
Budget-conscious buyers who still want it to look expensive

Brand Vibe
